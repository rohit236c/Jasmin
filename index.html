    About Us

    Established in the year 1995, in New Delhi, India, Jasmin, is one of the distinguished manufacturers, suppliers and exporters of purses. These are appreciated by domestic and international clients owing to features like perfect finish, shine, color, high tear strength and glossy appearance.

    With almost everything available to shop online now a day, the women handbag industry has emerged significantly over the internet, making it possible for every woman to purchase the handbags of her desired style and brand with great ease. The presences of different styles of designer women’s bags online can save you from the hassle to find the perfect bag by visiting different physical stores. Anyone can get women‘s bag online in India with few taps on their mobile screen and get them delivered right at your doorstep.
